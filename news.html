UWEC DECA at State

March 14, 2022

Small but mighty group of competitors at the Collegiate DECA state competition.

This past week DECA competed at the state competition in several catgories. The results of the event was great overall, as everyone who was competing placed in their respective events and will continue on to national later this year.At this state competition, 5 UWEC DECA members qualified to compete at the International Career Development Conference and placed among the top three in their events, including Business Ethics, Business-to-Business Marketing, International Marketing, Retail Management, and Sports and Entertainment Marketing. At the international conference in Arlington, members will compete with over 14,000 students from across the United States, Canada, and other countries.

DECA is a local, state, and international organization with a mission to prepare emerging leaders and entrepreneurs for careers in marketing, finance, hospitality, and management. Career focused students build skills through DECA competition, conferences and business mentoring.

Running for DECA Office

Jan 19, 2022

The deadline is approaching to start the process

The greatest thing about leadership within DECA is that there is infinite room for growth and there is equality of opportunity for all members. The classical progression of student leadership goes from chapter office to association office to executive office. If you’re questioning whether you should apply, here are some things to consider: When approaching a leadership position, it’s important to understand why you’re considering it in the first place. For many, leadership is a way to gain popularity and name recognition, build a resume and travel. But the best leaders are also driven by a desire to give back and serve others. DECA is a student-led organization committed to preparing emerging leaders around the world so a servant leadership mindset is more beneficial than an ego-centric approach. Please reach out the the exec board for more information.
