DECA is a international organization and has been focused on individual development of leadership and business skills. Is has been around for quite a while nationally, but for quite a bit of time on campus as well.

Here is a timeline of the organization. Today we focus on imporving the business skills and connection of our members though competion, speakers and civic engagement.

2021

Meeting are back in person with precautions. Traveled to state and ICDC in Madison and then in DC.

2020

COVID Pandemic afected meetings and competions. Everything went remote, meetings and competitons were quickly swapped to occur over zoom.

2004

UWEC Deca was created as a student organization on campus. Initally it was one of only a few buiness clubs on campus and secured less funding.

1946

With a 75-year history, DECA has impacted the lives of more than ten million students, educators, school administrators and business professionals since it was founded in 1946
